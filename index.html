Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a purchase?

Tap the '+' button in the top right in the Home or Purchases tab. Fill in information about your purchase, and optionally add a photo of the receipt.

How do I enable notifications?

Go to Settings → Notifications and turn on 'Enable notifications'. You can also customize when you want to receive notifications for each type of right.

Can I change the default country?

Yes, go to Settings → Default Settings → Default Country. This affects which laws and rules apply to your purchases.

What happens to my data?

All data is stored locally on your device. ValueGuard does not send any data to external servers. You have full control over your own data.
