ValueGuard Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 25, 2025

ValueGuard respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

ValueGuard stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transfer, or store any data on external servers. This includes:

  • Purchase information (name, amount, date)
  • Receipt images (stored locally)
  • Store name and information
  • Deadline settings
  • App settings

2. How We Use Data

All data is used exclusively locally on your device to:

  • Display purchase overview
  • Calculate deadlines
  • Send local notifications
  • Generate spending statistics

We never share your data with third parties.

3. Image Storage

Receipt images you take or select are stored only locally on your device. No images are uploaded or sent to external servers.

4. Notifications

Local notifications are scheduled and managed by the iOS system. We do not send push notifications from external servers.

5. iCloud Synchronization (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud synchronization in the future, data will be synchronized through your private iCloud account. Apple handles encryption and security for iCloud data. You can disable this feature at any time.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data from the app at any time through Settings → Data Management → Delete All Data. This cannot be undone.

7. Children

ValueGuard does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app requires no personal information and is safe for family use.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: dev_monkey@outlook.com
